Pan Grilled Hamburger with Balsamic Onions and Peppers
  • INGREDIENTS
  • DIRECTIONS
  1. 18g ground beef with 25% fat
  2. 1 hamburger bun
  3. romain lettuce as needed
  4. 1 large ripe tomato
  5. 1 medium sized yellow onion
  6. 1 red bell pepper
  7. 2tbsp balsamic vinegar
  8. salt
  9. Mayonnaise
  10. Dijon mustard
  11. fresh ground black pepper as needed
  1. Slice the onion and bell pepper into rings 1cm thick. Heat a sauté pan and drizzle in olive oil to coat the bottom of the pan. Once the oil is hot, add the onion and bell pepper and cook for about 3 minutes until the vegetables begin to soften. Add the balsamic vinegar and reduce to medium heat and cook until the balsamic vinegar has completely reduced and evaporated. Remove from the heat and season with salt and pepper. Set aside.
  2. Mix ground beef with your hands to form a patty. Season it with salt and pepper and set aside.
  3. Heat a non-stick grill pan over medium-high heat. Rub both sides of each hamburger patty with olive oil and place in the hot pan. Cook to desired doneness (about 2 to 3 minutes on each side for medium).
  4. While the patty is cooking slice the burger bun, tomato and prepare the lettuce.
  5. Once the hamburger is finished, place the patty on one of the buns and top with the balsamic onions and peppers. Serve with the lettuce, tomato, mustard and mayonnaise.
